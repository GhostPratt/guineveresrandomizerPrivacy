# Data Deletion Instructions for Guinevere's Randomizer

## How to Delete Your Data

### Option 1: Delete Data Within the App (Recommended)

**To delete your cloud-synced data:**
1. Open Guinevere's Randomizer app
2. Go to **Settings** tab
3. Tap **Sign Out**
4. Uninstall the app

This will:
- Stop cloud sync immediately
- Remove all local data from your device when you uninstall
- Cloud data will be automatically deleted after 30 days of inactivity

**To delete local data only:**
1. Go to your device's **Settings**
2. Navigate to **Apps** > **Guinevere's Randomizer**
3. Tap **Storage** > **Clear Data**
4. Or simply uninstall the app

### Option 2: Request Data Deletion via Email

If you need your data deleted immediately or cannot access the app, please email:

**Email:** guineveressoftware@gmail.com

**Subject:** Data Deletion Request - Guinevere's Randomizer

**Include in your email:**
- The email address you used to sign in to the app (if applicable)
- Your user ID (if known)
- Confirmation that you want all your data deleted

We will process your request within **30 days** and send you a confirmation email when complete.

## What Data Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, the following will be removed:

### Cloud Data (Firebase Firestore)
- Custom lists and list items
- Number range presets
- App settings and preferences
- All data synced to your account

### Local Data
- Lists stored on your device
- App preferences and settings
- Review tracking data
- Theme preferences

### Data We Cannot Delete
- Crash reports (anonymized, stored by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days)
- Subscription/payment records (required for legal/tax compliance, managed by Google Play)

## Data Deletion Timeline

- **Immediate:** Local data is deleted when you clear app data or uninstall
- **Within 30 days:** Cloud data is deleted after request is processed
- **Automatic:** Cloud data for inactive accounts (no sign-in for 30+ days) is eligible for deletion

## After Deletion

Once your data is deleted:
- You can still use the app locally without an account
- If you sign in again, you'll start with a fresh account
- Previously synced data will not be restored
- Subscription status is managed by Google Play and persists
